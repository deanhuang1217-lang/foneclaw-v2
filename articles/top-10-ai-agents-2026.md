## What Is an AI Agent?

An AI agent is software that can autonomously perform tasks by perceiving its environment, making decisions, and taking actions. Unlike simple chatbots, AI agents can:

- Multi-step task execution
- Learn from user behavior
- Control apps and devices
- Work across multiple platforms

The key difference? Traditional assistants answer questions. AI agents get things done.
